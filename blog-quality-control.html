Ensuring Quality: Our Advanced Testing Protocols

Learn about PharmaCorp's comprehensive quality control measures that ensure every product meets international standards and delivers safe, effective medications to patients worldwide.

At PharmaCorp, quality is not just a goalâ€”it's our fundamental commitment to every patient who depends on our medications. Our comprehensive quality control system ensures that every product leaving our facilities meets the highest international standards for safety, efficacy, and purity.

Multi-Stage Quality Control Process

Our quality assurance begins long before manufacturing and continues well after products reach the market. We employ a multi-stage approach that encompasses every aspect of pharmaceutical production:

Raw Material Testing

Every ingredient that enters our facilities undergoes rigorous testing:

  • Identity Verification: Advanced spectroscopic methods confirm the identity of each raw material
  • Purity Analysis: High-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC) ensures materials meet purity specifications
  • Microbial Testing: Comprehensive screening for harmful bacteria, yeast, and mold
  • Heavy Metal Detection: Atomic absorption spectroscopy checks for toxic metal contamination

In-Process Monitoring

During manufacturing, our quality control team conducts real-time monitoring:

  • Continuous environmental monitoring of temperature, humidity, and air quality
  • Regular sampling and testing at critical control points
  • Automated systems that halt production if parameters deviate from specifications
  • Documentation of every step for complete traceability

Advanced Testing Technologies

PharmaCorp has invested in state-of-the-art analytical equipment to ensure the highest level of quality control:

Analytical Instrumentation

  • HPLC-MS/MS Systems: For precise quantification of active ingredients and impurities
  • Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) Spectroscopy: For rapid identity confirmation
  • Dissolution Testing Equipment: To verify drug release profiles
  • Particle Size Analyzers: Ensuring optimal bioavailability
  • Stability Chambers: For accelerated and long-term stability studies

Regulatory Compliance

Our quality systems are designed to exceed regulatory requirements:

  • FDA cGMP Compliance: All facilities operate under current Good Manufacturing Practices
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certification: International quality management standards
  • WHO Prequalification: Meeting global health organization standards
  • Regular Audits: Internal and external audits ensure continuous compliance

Finished Product Testing

Before any product is released, it undergoes comprehensive final testing:

Release Testing Parameters

  • Assay testing to confirm active ingredient content
  • Dissolution testing to ensure proper drug release
  • Uniformity of dosage units
  • Microbial limits testing
  • Packaging integrity verification
  • Stability indicating methods

Continuous Improvement

Quality at PharmaCorp is an evolving process. We continuously invest in:

  • Advanced analytical methods development
  • Staff training and certification programs
  • Technology upgrades and automation
  • Process optimization based on data analysis
  • Collaboration with regulatory agencies and industry partners

Our Quality Promise

Every medication that bears the PharmaCorp name represents our unwavering commitment to quality. Our patients trust us with their health, and we honor that trust through rigorous testing, continuous monitoring, and an uncompromising dedication to excellence.

From the moment raw materials enter our facilities to the time our products reach patients, every step is guided by our quality-first philosophy. This commitment has earned us the trust of healthcare providers and patients across West Africa and beyond.
